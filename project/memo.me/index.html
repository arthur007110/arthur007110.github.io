Memo.me



The system was developed with the purpose of facilitating the sending of official documents between sectors, called memos, well as facilitating the sending and receiving of such documents. It will already make a good difference to public coffers and the environment.

The tool was developed by four students of the informatics course as their conclusion project, among the frameworks used are present: Node.js, Angular, PrimeNG, Firebase, Nodemailer, Quill, among others, the development of the tool started as a simple project of a web programming discipline but for its potential the tool went ahead, having its production beginning in mid-September 2018 and its completion in December 2019, when it was officially presented to the board of teachers who evaluated it, among the evaluators : Eduardo Wanderlley (Course Coordinator), Carlos Magno, Eugênio Saraiva.

The link for system access is: system-members.web.app was hosted on the firebase servers, nodemailer was deployed on a node.js server and later hosted on a heroku server.

With the development of this system was developed a great learning among the students involved, not only in the area of ​​programming and knowledge of technologies, but also in how to work in teams, using github.

Project Repository: https://github.com/arthur007110/Memo.me/
System link: https://sistema-memorandos.web.app/
